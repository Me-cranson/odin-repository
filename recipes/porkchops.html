Recipe name

Home

Description

Yummy yummy pork chops in some easy to make sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 pork chops
  • Spice mixture of flour, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, pepper, and salt
  • olive oil
  • brown sugar
  • butter
  • chicken stock
  • maple vinegar

Steps

  1. Season the meat well
  2. On medium high, cook for 2 minutes
  3. Sear the fat, then flip and cover for 5 minutes, low, testing for 145 degrees every 2 minutes
  4. Take out of pan, and fill with the maple vinegar, brown sugar, and chicken stock, stirring until reduced
  5. Remove from heat and add pork back in with butter
